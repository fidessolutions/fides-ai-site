Our Story

Like many young founders, we’ve built startups the hard way — juggling emails, replying to leads at midnight, and manually updating spreadsheets that never stayed in sync. We’ve wasted hours on tasks that felt endless, not because we wanted to, but because there wasn’t an affordable way to automate them.

Fides AI was born out of that frustration. This isn’t theory — it’s personal. We set out to create the tool we always needed: one that could replace the repetitive grind with intelligent, tailored automation. Now, we’re helping others do the same — saving time, reducing burnout, and giving small teams the superpowers they deserve.
